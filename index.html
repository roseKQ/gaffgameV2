This is Gaff
Gaff wants to help you learn about data. Will you accept the data mission?

what is my mission? Info for educators

A young family are moving to Belfast, and they don't know where they should live. Your mission is to use open data to find the perfect area for them to call home.

next step

Gaff has helped you get started on the mission by visiting open data NI and selecting three datasets on crime, parks and schools. Gaff has taken the JSON and .csv files and put them into a SQL database.

next step

You will identify the datatypes, find the primary and foreign keys and write SQL queries. Then you can visualise the data and help the family make an informed decision on where they should live.
